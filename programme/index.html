Around the British Library:

The Sir John Ritblat Treasures of the British Library Gallery tells the remarkable stories of over two thousand years of human experience. The exhibition, which is free to enter, includes magnificent hand-painted books from many faiths, maps and views, early printed books, literary, historical, scientific and musical works from around the world.
